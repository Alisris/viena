Celebrated premiere of THE PHANTOM OF THE OPERA

at the Raimund Theater

On Friday, March 15, 2024, Cameron Mackintosh's spectacular new production of THE PHANTOM OF THE OPERA celebrated its long-awaited premiere at the Raimund Theater. Andrew Lloyd Webber's musical masterpiece with world-famous songs such as "The Music of the Night", "Think of Me" and "The Masked Ball" and the legendary chandelier was met with great enthusiasm by the audience that evening.

The entire ensemble and the soloists were celebrated by the audience with frenetic applause and standing ovations. After the performance, the premiere and the upcoming season were celebrated n the Raimund Theater in a delighful atmosphere.
