Musicalclub

You love musicals and can't get enough of them?
Then the Musicalclub of Vereinigte Bühnen Wien is right for you!

 

As a Musicalclub member, you benefit from up to 50% discount when booking your tickets, receive a pre-purchase right at the start of sales, experience our backstage tours at a special price and more. You can find an overview of all the benefits under "Saving with the Musicalclub". 

 

Important news: the MUSICALCLUB will be redesigned from 2025!

The current MUSICALCLUB expires on 31 December 2024.
Your current membership will of course remain valid without restriction until this date. All existing benefits and discounts will continue to apply until 31 December 2024.

From 2 December 2024, you will have the opportunity to book membership of the redesigned MUSICALCLUB for the year 2025.
You can look forward to exciting new features, even more exclusive benefits and a flexible programme that accommodates both spontaneous ticket purchases and long-term planning.

BÜHNE magazine free of charge (issues in German language)*
Extraordinary reports and portraits, witty columns and sophisticated essays: 10 times a year, BÜHNE reports on everything that makes life exciting in front of and behind the scenes.
 

*Musicalclub members living in Austria, Germany and Switzerland will receive the BÜHNE magazine free of charge at the address you provided at your Musicalclub membership registration.   • How to get the discount

    Online you can make all bookings with your registered email address and password:

    • Visit the Onlineshop
    • Log in under "LOGIN" with your email address and password
    • Select the event you would like to attend
    • Select your seat
    • Select the "REDUCTIONS" button.
    • The tickets are already stored in your shopping cart with the Musicalclub discount
    • If they are not, select the "Musicalclub" discount category from the drop-down menu to adjust your reduction
    • Complete the order

     

    Please provide the email address that you gave when registering when you book by telephone to receive your discount. At the box offices, you will receive your discount if you provide the email address you gave when registering and show your identity card. There is no physical club card.

    Please note that the Musicalclub discounts only apply to our own productions at Raimund Theater and Ronacher. For third-party events, pricing is at the discretion of the organiser. The discounts apply to the categories VIP-D, the remaining tickets at € 22,- are available in the categories A-D .
