Groups & tour operators

Experience unforgettable musical theatre productions night after night in our historic theatres and immerse yourself in the spectacular world of musicals. Would you also like to inspire your customers with the unique magic of musicals? Our experienced VBW sales team is on hand with extensive expertise to help you create an extraordinary theatre experience for your customers. We will work with you to organise an unforgettable theatre experience - from an exclusive backstage tour to a visit to a performance and culinary delights during the interval. Put your trust in our experience and offer your guests an evening they won't soon forget.

Schools

Musical theatre is for everyone, young and old! Our aim is to awaken young people's interest in musical theatre and to inspire them with the musical and opera genres. With our varied offers, we want to bring children and young people closer to the magic of the theatre. 

In addition to attractive conditions for attending a performance, we offer backstage tours and workshops where they can try out musical theatre for themselves. We look forward to offering your pupils the opportunity to immerse themselves in the exciting world of musical theatre.
