PARTNERSHIP WORKS

Our Partners

 

Where dreams learn to fly, visions are given a stage and a world of virtuoso sounds is created, musical theatre takes place at the highest level. Vereinigte Bühnen Wien stages unforgettable musical theatre productions night after night. Enthusiastic partners, friends and sponsors contribute ideally and financially to the success of the three theatres and bring the willingness to bring magical moments to life together. VBW partners benefit from the positive image of the company and enjoy many advantages through their commitment. The Circle system with Platinum, Gold and Silver Circles offers an ideal platform for joint activities.

 

VEREINIGTE BÜHNEN WIEN would like to Thank:

WIENER STÄDTISCHE VERSICHERUNG

main sponsor

In their long-standing partnership, Vereinigte Bühnen Wien and Wiener Städtische are committed to promoting art and culture. The focus here is on children and young people, with the aim of sparking their interest in art and culture, making it more accessible and promoting young talent.

At Wiener Städtische's invitation, a total of 100 schoolchildren attend a musical production by Vereinigte Bühnen Wien every year. Forty of them then have the opportunity to work on a musical song with a dancer and a children's coach in an exciting workshop.

The choreography learned is then presented to parents, teachers, classmates and musical stars. It is always a very special highlight for the students to be able to prove themselves in front of the professionals.


CASINOS AUSTRIA 

ProduCtion sponsor 

On the 7th of October 2023, ROCK ME AMADEUS – THE FALCO MUSICAL, the latest in-house production by VBW, celebrated its eagerly awaited world premiere at the Ronacher. Vereinigte Bühnen Wien is delighted to present its long-standing partner CASINOS AUSTRIA as production sponsor.

VBW and CASAG are two Austrian companies with a high international profile. With its commitment, CASAG is making a valuable contribution to the realisation of this world premiere.
