The Orchestra of Vereinigte Bühnen Wien

 

The Orchestra of Vereinigte Bühnen Wien was founded in 1987. Today, it consists of 80 instrumentalists from a wide variety of genres, who have developed into a unified and highly acclaimed body of sound over the past decades. The orchestra plays first-class performances of musicals in the VBW theaters every evening and, due to its high quality, is also active in the jazz and big band field. Furthermore, it knows how to impress with film music interpretations as well as with its participation in operettas and modern chamber operas as well as numerous concert series at home and abroad.

  • Carsten Paap

    Conductor

    Carsten Paap studied church music, school music and music education at the University of Music Lübeck, majoring in organ, trombone and choral conducting, and graduated with distinction. He encountered the musical in 1997 at CATS in the Hamburg Operettenhaus, from where he continued in 1999 in Bremen with JEKYLL& HYDE as keyboardist and assistant musical director. In the same position he changed to MOZART! in Hamburg, where TITANIC - DAS MUSICAL and DIRTY DANCING followed. At the same time he conducted SONGS FOR A NEW WORLD on the Cap San Diego as well as ELEGIES FOR ANGELS, PUNKS AND RAGING QUEENS at the Schmidt Theater Hamburg and conducted the Berlin production of TANZ DER VAMPIRE. His work in Vienna includes REBECCA, MUSICAL CHRISTMAS, JESUS CHRIST SUPERSTAR, SISTER ACT, THE VISIT OF THE OLD LADY, MOZART!, MARY POPPINS, EVITA, DON CAMILLO & PEPPONE, I AM FROM AUSTRIA, BODYGUARD - THE MUSICAL, MISS SAIGON and THE LADY OF NOTRE DAME as well as musical direction for DANCE OF THE VAMPIRES and CATS. He was also Musical Director for the musical productions ANNIE (2012) and INTO THE WOODS(2014), which were created in collaboration with the Lower Austrian Music School Management. In May 2013 he was responsible for the premiere of the chamber opera JOIN! by Franz Koglmann at the Wiener Festwochen, and in September 2017 for the premiere of LIEBE SOPHIE and the EA of Anna Clyne's THIS MIDNIGHT HOUR (Vienna Radio Symphony Orchestra).

  • Herbert Pichler

    Conductor

    Herbert Pichler graduated from the University of Music and Performing Arts in Graz. He has been working as an artist in Vienna since 1989 and also teaches at the Institute for Popular Music at the University of Music and Performing Arts Vienna. He was musical director of MISS SAIGON, BARBARELLA, HAIR, MAMMA MIA! and BODYGUARD (all VBW), the new version of CABARET with Maria Bill (Volkstheater Wien) and the TV show "Ein Saitensprung" with Rainhard Fendrich. He has worked as a conductor on DER BAUER ALS MILLIONÄR (Landestheater Linz), "Musical!- Die Show" (ORF 2007) and I AM FROM AUSTRIA (VBW), among others. Since the first season of the ORF show "Dancing Stars", he has also been the conductor of the Star Orchestra. Other examples of his diverse musical activities include orchestral projects with Thomas Quasthoff, the mdw project CUBE, musical direction of the Nestroy Theatre Prize, collaboration with the Mozarteum Chamber Orchestra and several projects with Herbert Pixner and the Tonkünstler Orchestra. As an arranger and musician, Herbert Pichler has worked on CD productions and TV shows with artists such as Nina Hagen, Marianne Faithful and Chaka Khan, with whom he worked on the opening of the Vienna Festival in 2005, as well as with The Supremes, Harry Belafonte, Patricia Kaas, Placido Domingo, the Vienna Symphony Orchestra, the Vienna Radio Symphony Orchestra, Jos. Carreras, Luciano Pavarotti, Dave Stewart, Jim Steinman, Simply Red, Gloria Gaynor and many musical stars.

  • Michael Römer

    Conductor

    After studying in Bremen, Hanover and New York, he worked as a music educator, chamber musician, concert pianist and conductor. This was followed by solo performances with various orchestras and as a partner in chamber music ensembles, as well as recitals with soloists from various state operas; also with a focus on modern music (including collaboration with Karlheinz Stockhausen). As a conductor and répétiteur, he has worked on over 50 different (premiere) performances and premieres in Vienna and abroad. He has also been a guest lecturer at several musical schools and universities. Since the 2000/2001 season, he has been a permanent conductor at the Vereinigte Bühnen Wien, where he has been in charge of the rehearsals of numerous plays as well as the ongoing productions. In addition, he works as a pianist and consultant for many national and international auditions. Currently he is part of the music direction of the VBW. Most recently, he was Musical Director of WE ARE MUSICAL, the opening gala of the Raimund Theater and the concert performances of ELISABETH at Schönbrunn (2019 and 2022). As a supervisor, he oversees versions of ELISABETH, MOZART! and REBECCA in many countries for VBW, among others. In 2022/23, he will follow with the musical direction of Disney's THE HUNCHBACK at the Ronacher.

Als Gast wirkt er immer wieder bei Produktionen im Musiktheater Linz und im Tonkünstler-Orchester Niederösterreich mit.

Horst Hausleitner veröffentlichte seit 2008 drei Bücher, u. a. die Biografie der österreichischen Jazzlegende Hans Salomon „Jazz, Frauen und wieder Jazz“, die 2013 im Rahmen einer großen Gala im Ronacher präsentiert wurde.

    Flute, Clarinet, Saxophone

    Erik Schuster, born in 1991 in Neunkirchen, studied classical clarinet at the Joseph Haydn Conservatory Eisenstadt and jazz saxophone at the Music and Art Private University of the City of Vienna.

    Since then, Erik Schuster has established himself as a sought-after studio and live musician on the national and international music scene.

    The genres of the renowned multi-stylist include jazz, pop, funk, big band, classical, chamber music, orchestra, as well as brass music, folk music and Oberkrainer. Schuster not only plays the saxophone but also various clarinets and flutes, making him one of the most versatile reed players.

    Erik Schuster has performed with:
    Original Bill Haley`s Comets, Alfons Haider, Conchita Wurst, Frank Hoffmann, Jazz Gitti, Verena Scheitz, Bill Holman, Rex Richardson, Ack van Rooyen, Bart van Lier, Vienna Jazz Orchestra, Jazzophoniker, Broadway Big Band, siEbner Musi, and many more.

    Erik Schuster also enjoys his work as a music teacher of a clarinet and saxophone class in the music school association Bucklige Welt-Süd, Erik Schuster enjoys his work to the full. Many of his students are prize winners at ''prima la musica''.

    Erik Schuster is married and father of a daughter.

    He has been a member of the orchestra of the Vereinigte Bühnen Wien since 2022.
