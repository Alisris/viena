WE ARE MUSICAL: Big musical festival to kick off the season at the Ronacher
Saturday, September 21, 12:00 - 17:00 pm

 

 

The Ronacher opened its doors on 21 September 2024! Just in time for the start of the season, Vereinigte Bühnen Wien was once again organising the big musical festival WE ARE MUSICAL around the musical theatre in the 1st district.

 

Open-air stage, show highlights, face painting for children and much more

On the open-air stage, audience favourites Carin Filipčić, Marjan Shaki, James Park and Drew Sarich performed the best VBW musical songs. The programme also included a musical dance & musical sing-along. Robert Steiner and the popular rat ‘Rolf Rüdiger’ were once again the presenters. 

Show highlights from the current VBW success production ROCK ME AMADEUS - THE FALCO MUSICAL as well as dance workshops, workshop talks, face painting for children and further exciting programme from the world of theatre were waiting for the visitors.

At the souvenir flea market, all musical lovers could get merchandise from past productions. A food truck in front of the Ronacher spoiled visitors with various delicious dishes.

 

The musical festival took place as part of Wien Holding's Open Day. Details can be found here!
