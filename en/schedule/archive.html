    Dutch Divas - The Concert

    14.10.2024 - 14.10.2024

    Three fascinating voices - three Dutch women - three extraordinary personalities - a gala concert of superlatives!

    They are known from the most famous musicals of the last decades and now they were performing together on one stage for the very first time:  Ana Milva Gomes, Annemieke Van Dam and Wietske Van Tongeren.

    They were supported on stage by none other than musical superstars Mark Seibert and Drew Sarich. They were accompanied by a 6-piece live band under the musical direction of Herbert Pichler.

    THE HUNCHBACK OF NOTRE DAME

    Experience the successful musical at the Ronacher!

    08.10.2022 - 25.06.2023

    ~ 2:40h

    Disney's THE HUNCHBACK OF NOTRE DAME promises an extraordinary and highly moving musical experience for young and old with all the unforgettable songs of the world-famous film. This impressive musical adaptation captivates not only with its Oscar-nominated, opulent music featuring large church choirs, but also with Scott Schwartz's lavish and powerful staging. The successful play performed after many international productions, including in the USA, Berlin and Tokyo, finally for the first time in Vienna at the Ronacher in 2022 and 2023.
