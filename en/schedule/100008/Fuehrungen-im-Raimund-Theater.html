Take A LOOK BEHIND THE SCENES!

Guided tours of the Raimund Theater

 


Not only coffee houses, Fiaker & Schnitzel characterise Viennese culture, but also the diverse theatre landscape is an essential part of our city. The Raimund Theater's true musical triumph began after its general refurbishment in the mid-1980s and its integration into the Vereinigte Bühnen Wien (VBW). A balanced mixture of international world hits and VBW's own productions offers the audience a varied and impressive theatre experience every evening. As part of a backstage tour, you will learn about the history of the theatre, enter the stage and take a look at the elaborate technical facilities that are otherwise not on display. Join us on a tour of our theatre, change your perspective and be inspired by the exciting world of theatre operations.

  • Duration: approx. 1 hour (no intermission)

  • In German

  • Number of participants: max. 25 persons

 

Please note:

our backstage tours are not barrier-free.

 

Our tours start at the main entrance of the theater in Wallgasse 18-20, 1060 Vienna.

 


We are happy to organize individual tours for you and your group!

Please address your request to Sandra Swoboda: sandra.swoboda@vbw.at
