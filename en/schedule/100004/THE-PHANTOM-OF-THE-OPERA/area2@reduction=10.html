  • Senior-Ticket

    Seniors aged 60 and over receive a 20% discount for selected performances (marked with a blue dot) in the categories VIP-D marked with the blue dot (max. 2 tickets per person can be booked, both must fulfil the requirements for the senior discount - random checks of the age limit on the day of the performance).

    MORE INFORMATION

  • Junior-Ticket

    Children and young people up to the age of 16 accompanied by at least one adult receive a 35% discount in the categories VIP-D for selected performances (marked with an orange dot). The Junior-Ticket can be used by a maximum of 3 children accompanied by an adult. The discount can also be booked in combination with a discounted adult ticket.

    MORE INFORMATION

  • Physically Challenged

    Wheelchair with accompaniment:

    Available from all VBW Box Offices and telephonically at WIEN-TICKET +43 1 58885 - 111. Book at least one week before the desired performance at only € 10,- for Wheelchair admission. Your companion will recieve a 50% reduction on the ticket price. Available for the categories A-D.

     

    Physically challenged:

    50% discount upon presentation of a valid "Disability ID Pass". A registered companion (B, Begleitperson erforderlich) may also recieve a 50% redution on the ticket fare. A discounted ticket for non-registered companions is not possible. Available for the categories VIP Gold-D.

Guided Tour Raimund Theater

Take a look behind the scenes!

As part of a backstage tour, you will learn a lot about the history of the house, step onto the stage and take a look at the otherwise concealed complex technical facilities. Join us on our way through our house, change your perspective and let yourself be inspired by the exciting world of theater business.

Please note:

  • Our tours take about an hour and have no break.
  • The tours are held in German. English tours are currently not available.
  • The number of participants is limited to a maximum of 25 persons for security reasons.
  • Our backstage tours are not barrier-free.
  • Our tours start at the main entrance of the theater  in Wallgasse 18-20, 1060 Vienna.
  • We are happy to organize individual tours for you and your group. Please address your request to Sandra Swoboda: sandra.swoboda@vbw.at
