Andrew Lloyd Webber’s masterpiece at the Raimund Theater

THE PHANTOM OF THE OPERA

 

One of the most famous musicals of all time is finally back in Vienna! After the great success of CATS, the Vereinigte Bühnen Wien are presenting another musical world hit by composer Andrew Lloyd Webber. Experience Cameron Mackintosh's spectacular new production of Andrew Lloyd Webber's THE PHANTOM OF THE OPERA, which has never before been seen in German-speaking countries, at the Raimund Theater. 

  • Duration: approx. 2 hours 30 minutes

    (one intermission)

  • In German with English Surtitles

  • Age recommendation: from 10 years

    Andrew Lloyd Webber has composed some of the world's most famous musicals, and his shows have now been running in London's West End without interruption for 50 years. Lloyd Webber is passionate about the importance of music education and diversity in the arts.
