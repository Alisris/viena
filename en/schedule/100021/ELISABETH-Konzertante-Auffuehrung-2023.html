ELISABETH - IN CONCERT 2023

Open Air at the Ehrenhof of Schloss Schönbrunn

Due to the enormous audience success and a total of over 30,000 tickets sold, the VBW’s internationally successful production ELISABETH, penned by the successful duo of Michael Kunze & Sylvester Levay, was performed also from the 29th of June to the 1st of July 2023 as an exceptional concert experience in the grand forecourt of Schönbrunn Palace.

The world hit as a great concert highlight at the original location - the international VBW successful production ELISABETH by the successful duo Michael Kunze & Sylvester Levay as a really great concert experience of the extra class in front of the Schönbrunn Palace! The famous historical setting offers a worthy and unique ambience for the concert version of the musical, which tells the dramatic and touching story about the life, work and suffering of Empress Elisabeth of Austria.

With top-class principals and ensemble, accompanied by the orchestra of the Vereinigte Bühnen Wien, the concert version of the most successful German-language musical of all time will be staged in the original setting in 2023.
